Steps to Make Yummy Vegetable Salad

  1. Choose Your Base Greens Romaine, spinach, arugula, or mixed baby greens Wash thoroughly and dry (wet greens = soggy salad).
  2. Include Juicy & Sweet Elements Tomatoes (cherry or chopped), corn kernels, shredded beetroot Optional: fruits like mango, apple, or orange segments.
  3. Layer in Flavor Boosters Red onion, spring onion, olives, pickled jalapeños Herbs: fresh dill, basil, mint, or coriander.
  4. Add Protein (Optional) Boiled eggs, chickpeas, tofu cubes, grilled paneer For non-vegetarian: grilled chicken or tuna.
  5. Toss & Taste Use a large bowl to gently toss all ingredients Adjust seasoning—don’t forget a pinch of salt!.
  6. Serve Fresh Best served immediately to preserve crunch Garnish with seeds (sunflower, pumpkin) or crushed nuts.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
